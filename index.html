Hazards and Helpers

A festive game of chance

Can you help Santa on his journey to deliver his presents on Christmas Eve? He has 1000 miles to travel.

Roll the dice and Santa will move on 100 miles. If your dice roll totals more than 10 he will meet a helper, who will boost Santa's energy. If your dice roll totals 10 or less then he will encounter a hazard who will reduce his energy.

Beware that Santa's maximum energy level is 60.

Will Santa reach the children's stockings before he runs out of energy?

Press the PLAY button to start the game.
